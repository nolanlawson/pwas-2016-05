Alex Russell

What makes a progressive web app?

  • Native-like characteristics
  • Promoted by browser chrome

Native-like characteristics

  • Works offline
  • Launches from home screen
  • Hides URL bar
  • Branded colors
  • "First class" treatment in OS
  • Push notifications (optional)
  • Background sync (optional)
